About Rishi Chheda Academy

Founded by Rishi Chheda, RCA is a premier coaching institute dedicated to shaping the next generation of Chartered Accountants. We specialize in CA Foundation, Intermediate & Final preparation with a laser focus on conceptual clarity, exam strategy, and disciplined learning.

Our philosophy is simple — every student deserves personalized attention and a clear roadmap to success. With small batch sizes, expert faculty, and a results-driven curriculum, we've helped hundreds of students clear their CA exams with flying colors.
